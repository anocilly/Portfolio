# Wind Turbine Research

In Spring 2026, I had the opportunity to work as a research assistant for Prof. Rebecca Barthlelmie's Wind Energy Research Lab at Cornell University. My research focused on the leading edge erosion (LEE) of wind turbine blades, a critical issue that affects the efficiency and longevity of wind turbines.
Much of the work is covered by an NDA, but attached is my research summary that I submitted to Prof. Barthelmie at the end of the semester.
I also received the Bart Conta Prize in Energy and the Environment for my work on this project, which is awarded to undergraduate and graduate students in MAE for the best work on a research or design project dealing with energy and the environment.
